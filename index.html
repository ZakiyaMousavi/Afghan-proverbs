Afghan Proverbs

Afghan Proverbs are deeply important in Afghan culture.They reflect the values, wisdom and experiences of the people. These proverbs are not just sayings, they are tools for commucinatio, teaching the values and even conflict resolution.

Featured Proverbs:


  • قطره قطره جمع گردد، وانگهی دریا شود
  • Drop by drop, a river is made.
  • Meaning: Small efforts add up to big results.
  • ادم تنبل، عقل چهل وزیر را دارد.
  • A lazy person has the wisdom of forty ministers.
  • Meaning: A lazy person could outsmart forty wise men- just to skip a chore!
