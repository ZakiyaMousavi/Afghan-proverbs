  • اب در کوزه و ما تشنه لبان می گردیم
  • Water is in the jug, but we wander around thirsty!

Meaning:This proverb points to the irony of seeking something far away while it's actually right beside us. Like when we search everywhere for solutions, love or help while the answer is already close by.

  • اب که از سر گذشت, چه یک نیزه, چه صد نیزه
  • Once the water has passed over the head, what difference is one one spear or a hundred?

    • Meaning:This proverb means that when the worst has already happened, additional damage or punishment does not matter anymore.

  • اب از اب تکان نخوردن
  • The water did not even shake.

    • Meaning: Not even a ripple moved in the water, it is often used to describe a situation where something serious happened, but nothing changed, there was no reaction or everything stayed calm and still.

  • اب رفته به جوی بر نمی گردد.
  • The water that has flowed away does not return to the stream.

    • Meaning: This proverb indicates that once something is lost, like time, opportunity, or spoken words, it can't be brought back.
